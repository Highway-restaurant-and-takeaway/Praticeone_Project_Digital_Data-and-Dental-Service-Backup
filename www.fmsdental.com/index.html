NUERO-MUSCULAR DENTISTRY & TMD CLINIC

 The Orofacial Pain Specialists at FMS, deals with TMD (temporomandibular joint disorders) and atypical jaw, head and/or neck pains etc. At FMS, the most advanced Nuero-Muscular Dentistry Clinic is equipped with T-SCAN Measured Occlusion, 3D Jaw Tracker, Electromyography, Joint Vibration Analysis,Tens Therapy etc. T-SCAN is the only clinically recognized and validated digital occlusal analysis system that helps to identify premature contact, high forces and the interrelationship of occlusal surfaces, thereby balancing your occlusion to precision. Read More >>>

ALIGN YOUR TEETH INVISIBLY

Page 3s, models, working class, here’s good news for you… get your crooked teeth corrected with the all new invisible aligners with out getting noticed by using Clear Aligners. These are a series of custom made plastic aligners created based on the digital models made from the impression or a scan from your teeth. These aligners are based on the simulated tooth movements and provide a gradual series of correction in order to achieve the full alignment. With these advantages the aligners are best suited for patients with aesthetic concern. Read More >>>
