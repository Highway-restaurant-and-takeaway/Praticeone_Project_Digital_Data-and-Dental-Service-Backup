Your smile is very important.  It is one of the first things you notice when you meet someone and also one of the first things that impress someone. Smile is not only about the lips, but also the teeth and gums behind it, including the jaw line. Best Cosmetic Dental Clinic in India

Once the hallmark of the stars, the rich, and the famous, Cosmetic Dentistry is now easily accessible to each one of us. While traditional dentistry focuses on oral hygiene and preventing, diagnosing, and treating oral disease, cosmetic dentistry focuses on improving a person’s overall appearance giving a complete new look.

With modern advances in dentistry, cosmetic procedures can range from tooth colour enhancements, laminates, composite bonding, correction of the misalignment of the teeth etc. to digitally design your overall smile. The changes brought by various modalities of Dental procedures will give you that wow look! 

So, walk into the Face Makeover and Smile Clinics and pamper yourselves with a best cosmetic dentist, smile designing, dental porcelain veneers, smile makeover procedures, that can Gift you an FMS DESIGNER SMILE. Best Cosmetic Dental Clinic in India

What makes your Smile Beautiful?

 

  • TEETH – color, size, shape, position & alignment to face / lips
  • GUMS  – color, shape, position & visibility
  • LIPS     – length, shape & size
  • FACE   – shape, form & asymmetry

How do we plan your smile?

 

  • Clinical Evaluation of your smile
  • Professional Photography / Videography
  • Evaluation of facial form & symmetry
  • Digital Smile Designing & Virtual Planning using Softwares
  • Understanding your  Personal Expectations 

SMILE DESIGNING – a Multidisciplinary approach ?

Teeth

  • Whitening with Simple bleaching
  • Shaping with Enamel Contouring
  • Masking with Composite Veneers
  • Laminates to alter shape, size, color
  • Aligning with Minor Orthodontics

Gums

  • LASER Depigmentation for dark gums
  • Gum Surgery for triangular spaces between teeth
  • Gum Contouring for shaping gums
  • Gum Surgery for Exposed Roots
  • Thinning of bulbous gums

Lips

  • Neuro modulator injection for Lip Lengthening
  • Fillers for shaping Lips
  • Surgery for short / long lips
  • Combined procedures

Face

  • Fillers & Neuro modulator for asymmetry
  • Surgical corrections
  • LASERs & Skin Care
