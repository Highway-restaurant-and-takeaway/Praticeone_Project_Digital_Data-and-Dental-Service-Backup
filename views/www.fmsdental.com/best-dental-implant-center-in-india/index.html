CLICK HERE TO SEE THE CENTER
  • FMS INTERNATIONAL DENTAL CENTER, JUBILEE HILLS and FMS Dental Hospital, Koti, Hyderabad, are the exclusive Centers for Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery and Dental Implants and awarded as the Best Dental Implant Center in India. The FMS DENTAL KOTI facility is equipped with an Operation theatre, Dental Implant clinic, I.C.U and In-patient facility, along with a team of Implantologists, Maxillofacial Surgeons, Anesthetist and Paramedical staff. This Implant center also deals with all kinds of dental emergencies, with Maxillofacial surgeons available on-call, round the clock.
  • On consultation with the FMS Implant Team, the right implant solution suitable for each situation, will be explained. FMS Dental is definitely one of the pioneers in dental implants, in India. All the renowned implant systems in the world are available under one roof, viz Nobel Biocare (Sweden), Branemark (Sweden), Alpha Bio (England), Alpha Dent (Netherlands), Biohorizons (USA), KOS / BCS / BOI Single Piece Implants (IDHE Dental Switzerland), Gen XT Single Piece Implants (Switzerland) and many more…..
  • This Best Dental Implant Center in India, is also equipped with world-class Dental LASER systems, to help Implant procedures make simpler, painless, bacteria-free as and when required.
  • Routinely Dental Implant procedures are performed under Local Anesthesia, in the most experienced hands. The Operation Theater – Dental Implant Clinic is equipped for General Anesthesia and Conscious Sedation facility, for complex cases of implants/bone grafting procedures and also for Uncooperative/ Dental Phobia patients.
  • The CAD-CAM supported Hi-tech Dental lab attached to Implant Center, is equipped with the all new SIRONA CAD-CAM system with Mill from Germany and the PROCERA CAD-CAM system from Sweden, for Metal-free Zirconium and Alumina based Ceramic Crowns & Bridges, with an astounding precision and level of aesthetics.

 

Sterilization and Infection Control
  • FMS Dental adheres The OSHA (Occupational Safety and Health Act) regulations for sterilization and infection control. All the centers are equipped with Class-B autoclaves and DOC autoclaves (Handpiece) for sterilization. Sterilization begins right at our door step. Routine fumigation of the clinics and liberal use of disposables ensures the safety of the patients in every procedure.
